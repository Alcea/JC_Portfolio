About Me

Jodie Chang enjoys adding narrative/symbolic concepts that drive her overall design. The first programming language she learned was Python which then branched off to other languages such as Java, Processing and C#. Design wise, she is experienced with the Adobe Suite specifically: Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects and Premiere. Jodie is currently an undergraduate student enrolled in Simon Fraser University’s School of Interactive Arts and Technology faculty to obtain a bachelor in arts with a concentration in media arts. This field of study teaches designers to be multidisciplinary with the ability to prototype and develop their own projects.
