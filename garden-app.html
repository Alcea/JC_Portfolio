Garden to Table

Jodie Chang is currently an undergraduate student enrolled in Simon Fraser University’s School of Interactive Arts and Technology faculty to obtain a bachelor in arts with a concentration in media arts. This field of study teaches designers to be multidisciplinary with the ability to prototype and develop their own projects. Hence, she has proficient skills in both object-oriented programming and programs in the Adobe Suite specifically: Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects and Premiere. Concept driven design is a process that she prioritizes regardless if it’s a Unity game project that she’s working on or graphic design, UI/UX projects.
